Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

FirmMatch SG ("we", "us", "our") is a matching and referral platform connecting Singapore SMEs with accounting, bookkeeping, tax, payroll and audit service providers. We operate in accordance with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

2. Data We Collect

When you submit an enquiry or partner application, we collect:

  • Company name and UEN
  • Contact person name, email address, and phone number
  • Industry, revenue range, and business volume information
  • Services requested
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We do not collect NRIC/FIN numbers, bank account details, financial statements, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To review and process your service enquiry
  • To match you with up to 3 suitable service providers
  • To share a summary of your requirements with matched firms for quotation purposes
  • To communicate with you about your enquiry and quotes received

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond matching and referral.

4. How We Share Your Data

Your information will be shared only with up to 3 matched service providers for the purpose of providing you with a quotation. These firms are required to use your data solely for preparing and submitting a quote.

We do not sell, rent, or otherwise disclose your personal data to any third party beyond the matched firms.

Your full contact details are only released to a partner firm once you have indicated interest in their quotation.

5. Data Retention

We retain your enquiry data for up to 12 months from the date of submission, after which it is securely deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 7).

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to enquiry data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

7. Your Rights

Under the PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (note: withdrawal will mean we cannot process your enquiry)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello.firmmatchsg@gmail.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality (e.g. remembering form state). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries or data requests, contact us at:
Email: hello.firmmatchsg@gmail.com

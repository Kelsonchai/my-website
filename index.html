FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Is FirmMatch SG free to use?

Yes, completely free for businesses. You submit your request, we match you with up to 3 firms, and you receive quotes at no cost. We earn a referral fee from the firms, not from you.

How long does it take to receive quotes?

Most clients receive quotes within 1–2 business days after submission. In some cases, complex requests (e.g. audit) may take up to 3 business days.

Do I have to accept any of the quotes?

No obligation at all. You compare the quotes and only engage a firm if you're happy with the proposal. There is no commitment required.

Who are the firms on your platform?

All partner firms are Singapore-registered, verified, and have experience serving SME clients. Firms offering audit services are ACRA-registered Public Accounting Entities or Public Accountants.

Will my information be kept confidential?

Your details are only shared with the matched firms for quotation purposes. We do not sell your data. You can request deletion of your data at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

What if I need more than one service?

No problem. You can select multiple services in your request (e.g. bookkeeping + GST + payroll). We'll match you with firms that can handle the full scope.

Does my company need to be GST-registered?

No. We serve both GST-registered and non-GST-registered companies. Simply indicate your GST status in the request form and we'll match accordingly.

I'm a new company. Can I still use the platform?

Yes. We work with newly incorporated companies and startups. Let us know in the form and we'll match you with firms experienced in onboarding new businesses.
